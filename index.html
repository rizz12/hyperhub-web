How Governance Works in Hyperliquid

Hyperliquid governance occurs through HIP (Hyperliquid Improvement Proposals). HYPE holders vote on changes like fee structure or staking rewards. To vote, you must have staked HYPE to a validator – vote delegation is automatic. If you want to change your vote, restake to another validator (e.g., from val1 to val2), which transfers your vote. Current quorum: 51% of staked HYPE. More at docs.hyperliquid.xyz.

Tip: Restake via wallet (MetaMask + Hyperliquid app) – fee ~0.01 HYPE, instant.

HSI Index Details

The HSI is a composite index that measures overall sentiment in Hyperliquid. It integrates multiple factors for a holistic view. This is crucial for predicting market momentum. High scores often precede price increases. Use it to gauge entry points.

GIYP Details

GIYP predicts yield impacts from governance. It helps stakers optimize returns. Understanding vote outcomes is key for long-term holding. This metric can guide restaking decisions. It's important for risk-adjusted yields.

ADL Risk Heatmap Details

ADL visualizes leverage risks. Red areas signal potential liquidations. This prevents wipeouts in volatile markets. Monitor it during high-volume periods. Essential for leveraged traders.

WFSI Details

WFSI tracks whale movements and sentiment. High scores indicate accumulation. Whales often lead market trends. Use this to follow smart money. Critical for momentum trading.

AOS Details

AOS scans for arbitrage opportunities. High scores mean profitable spreads. This exploits inefficiencies across chains. Important for HFT strategies. Monitor for quick profits.

SMS Details

SMS measures social hype. Spikes predict pumps. Social momentum drives volume. Track it for sentiment shifts. Vital for retail-driven markets.

LST Yield Optimizer Details

LST optimizes staking yields. Recommends best options. Adjusts for risk. Maximizes passive income. Key for long-term holders.

CCLB Details

CCLB benchmarks latency. Highlights Hyperliquid advantages. Lower latency means better HFT. Compare with competitors. Essential for speed-sensitive trades.

MMIA Details

MMIA alerts on imbalances. Bearish if takers dominate. Affects liquidity. Monitor for market health. Important for makers.
