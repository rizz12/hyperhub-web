HSI Breakdown

The Hyperliquid Sentiment Index tracks 5 key metrics to gauge market health.

Social Momentum (20%) Tracks X/Reddit buzz and keyword frequency for hype signals.
Whale Activity (30%) Monitors large wallet transfers (> $100k) and accumulation patterns.
OI Ratios (20%) Open interest long/short balance vs price action.
Funding Rates (15%) Perpetual funding rates indicating bullish/bearish leverage bias.
Volume Flows (15%) Trading volume trends compared to 30-day moving averages.
